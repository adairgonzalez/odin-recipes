Fish Tacos

Description

Love fish tacos? Well, these are the end-all be-all.

Read on to see what makes these tacos so special.

Ingredients

  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Chili powder
  • Paprika
  • Ground cumin
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Cod
  • Vegetable oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Corn tortillas
  • Avocado
  1. In a medium bowl, whisk olive oil, paprika, chili powder, cumin, and cayenne together.
  2. Add cod, tossing until eventually coated. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. In a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, heat vegetable oil. Remove cod from marinade and season both sides of each fillet with salt and pepper.
  4. Add fish flesh side-down.
  5. Cook until opaque and cooked through, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
  6. Let rest 5 minutes before flaking with a fork
  7. Assmble the tacos. Serve fish over grilled tortillas with avocado and prefered toppings.
  8. Enjoy!
