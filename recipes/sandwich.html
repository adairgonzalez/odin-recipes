Sandwich

Description

Do you sometimes just want a plain sandwich? Well, you're in luck if you forgot how to make one.

Read on to see how to make these really complicated sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Turkey Ham
  • Classic Bologna
  • Yellow American Cheese
  • Avocado
  1. In a plate, place the two pieces of bread and cover them with mayonnaise slightly.
  2. Place the ham on top of one of the bread slices. This slice will be where you place the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Add one slice of bologna on top.
  4. Continue with a piece of yellow American cheese on top of the bologna.
  5. This is optional, but if you like avocado, feel free to cut some slice and place them on top of the cheese.
  6. Grab the other unoccupied slice of bread, and place it on top of the other one with the ingredients.
  7. Admire the beautiful piece of art you just created and enjoy!
